A Tiny Bit About Me

I am a friendly East London based web developer who loves technology and has found a passion in the form of code and design of fast and responsive websites. I have been a techy for as long as I can remember and my belief is that having a solid web presence provides the foundations for a successful business and having a website means spreading the word, customers are always able to find you anytime, anywhere.

My own experiences in tech have molded my ability to create lightweight, fast and responsive websites as such I now offer a limited freelance service, ranging from logo and responsive website design and development to website critique and testing. Feel free to contact me about tech positions and collaboraton work as I am currently seeking new opportunities.
